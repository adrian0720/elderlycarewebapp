Oximeter Watch

Brand: HealthTech

Model: HT-2000

The Oximeter Watch monitors blood oxygen levels, providing real-time data to ensure the health and well-being of seniors, facilitating proactive care.

Features:

  • Continuous SpO2 Monitoring: Tracks blood oxygen saturation levels throughout the day.
  • Data Sync: Connects to mobile apps for data tracking and analysis.

Monitoring Camera

Brand: SecureView

Model: SV-1080P

Smart cameras help improve senior care with remote monitoring and supervision, ensuring safety, security, and well-being of the elderly.

Features:

  • High-Definition Video: Offers clear live feeds for caregivers and family.

Pulse Watch

Brand: VitalSense

Model: VS-500

The Pulse Watch tracks heart rate and pulse, enabling caregivers to monitor vital signs and respond quickly to any health changes in elderly individuals.

Features:

  • Heart Rate Monitoring: Provides real-time heart rate tracking.
